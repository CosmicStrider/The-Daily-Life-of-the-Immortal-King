Especially since the capture of Evil Sword God. According to the research of the overseas criminal pursuit team at the cultivation Police Station, the pursuit team is sure that they have never come back.

The capture of Evil Sword God is a warning to them. But just because he hasn't come back at this stage doesn't mean he won't find an opportunity to come back in the future.

The cultivation Police Station has been worried that maybe the Four Elephants are planning some big plan. For example, they could rescue the Evil Sword God after a prison robbery.

This is what the Cultivation Police Department is most worried about. A prison break is not the worst thing; what they fear is that the people who break in are all madmen, and madmen who have a plan.

If they really decide to set out to rescue the Evil Sword God, then the pressure on the cultivation Police Station will be even greater.

Therefore, instead of feeling anxious all the time, it is better to take action early and capture several people together.

After the capture is completed, the task of assisting the development of Wang Ling and Sun Rong will naturally become the next main task.

You would never imagine how cautious a group of extremely vicious criminals could be at this juncture.
